Salary Calculation Rules
Attendance Rules:
  • On Time: Full day count (1.0)
  • Late (< 1 hour): Full day but penalty applies
  • Very Late (1+ hour): Half day count (0.5)
  • Absent (no leave): 2 days salary cut
  • Approved Leave: 1 day salary cut
Penalty Rules:
  • 3 Late marks = 0.5 day cut
  • 1 Absent day = 2 days cut
  • 1 Hour+ late = Half day (0.5)
  • 1 Leave = 1 day cut
Formula:

Effective Days = Present Days - Late Penalties - Absent Penalties - Leaves
Final Salary = Effective Days × (Base Salary ÷ 30)
