Holiday Management Info
How Holidays Work:
  • Holidays are NOT counted as absent - No penalty applied
  • Employees don't need to punch in/out on holidays
  • Salary is NOT deducted for holiday days
  • Multi-day holidays supported (e.g., Diwali 3 days)
  • Can be activated/deactivated anytime
Examples:
  • Republic Day: 26 Jan (1 day)
  • Diwali: 10 Nov to 12 Nov (3 days)
  • Christmas: 25 Dec (1 day)
