How Week Off Works
Week Off Rules:
  • Week off days are NOT counted as absent - No penalty applied
  • Employees don't need to punch in/out on week off days
  • Salary is NOT deducted for week off days
  • Multiple days can be set as week off (e.g., Saturday + Sunday)
  • Changes apply immediately to all employees
Example:

If Sunday is marked as week off:
- Month has 4 Sundays = 4 days
- These 4 days will NOT be counted in absent calculation
- Employee salary will NOT be affected by these days
